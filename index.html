Welcome to Kalimantan Timur

About

Kalimantan Timur is a a province located on the east of the Pulau Kalimantan aka Borneo Island due to its strategic location that isn't directly exposed to the ocean nor located in the ring of fire the province Kalimantan Timur is a very safe region with little to no recorded dangerous disaster--such as earthquake--in the last decade.
